Cookies

Cookies may be used to collect non-personal information and improve the overall experience of the website. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you. Our advertising partners deliver some advertisements that appear on this Site. Our advertising partners may download cookies to your computer. which allows the advertising network to recognize your computer each time they send you an advertisement. In this way, they may compile information about where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisements and determine which advertisements are clicked. This information allows an advertising network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. Career Advance does not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by the third party advertisers.
